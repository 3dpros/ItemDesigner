Covid-19 Shield Project (Austin, TX)

We are building face shields for local medical professionals using a 3D printed headpiece and a thin plastic laminate shield.  These are intended to be easily producible, and provide good protection while being able to produce as many as possible.

Recommended Print Settings:

  • PETG Material - can be sterilized with bleach, and does not have known skin reactions
  • thick layers (~.3 for .4mm nozzle) to maximize speed. Can increase further with larger nozzles
  • 245C nozzle temp to improve strength
